Banana Pudding

Description

Homemade no-cook banana pudding.

Ingredients

  • Bananas
  • Milk
  • Vanilla pudding mix
  • Sweetened condensed milk
  • Vanilla extract
  • Frozen whipped topping
  • Vanilla wafers

Steps

  1. Whisk pudding mix into milk
  2. Add sweetened condensed milk and vanilla
  3. Fold in frozen whipped topping
  4. Layer mixture with sliced bananas and vanilla wafers
